Dad Rising: A Family Adventure

Hello, Emmanuel!

You have now arrived at the family's summer house. Your parents stay here for a few weeks.
The inevitable has however happened and your dad has been rehearsing his classical jokes for weeks, waiting for a moment of weakness to knock you or worse, your mom, partner or children, out, with a joke you've heard your whole life.
He could be lurking anywhere, ready to crack anyone up!
Will you be able to save them in time?
Will you be able to survive? Only time will show... Good luck!
